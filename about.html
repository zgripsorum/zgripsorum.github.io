About Us

Zgripsorum is a Romanian Melodic Death Metal band formed in 2019.
Fusing melodic and symphonic elements with the rawness of old school Death Metal, we create a beautifully chaotic new realm.

We started out as a 2-men band but we quickly managed to find a drummer. The beginning was quite slow, as we wrapped our heads around too many songs at the same time. In the summer of 2019 we posted a short demo of a song of ours on our Instagram page. It became popular quickly between the 100 followers we had at that time and we got really excited. Sadly, the song was never to be finished, because of the lack of ideas.

We then started composing our first single, "Gai'don", releasing a demo for it in April 2020. The success was immense, so we worked and released the full version with our new vocalist in July.

Members:

-Rares Andreescu (Rhythm Guitar)
-Catalin Chivu (Bass Guitar/Clean and Backing Vocals)
-Matei Cochintu (Main Vocals)
-Octav Oprinoiu (Lead Guitar)

*Fun facts

  • "Gai'don" was originally named "Elegy of Shadows". This was before we even thought about any lyric.
  • The title "Exile" is actually inspired by the game "Path of Exile", a game with a similar story to our lyrics.
  • Because of the very long writing session of our first single, at the time we released it we already hated it.
  • (Sad Fact) Given the current pandemic, we were able to rehearse twice in a year.
