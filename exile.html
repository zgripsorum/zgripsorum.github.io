Exile

by Zgripsorum

Support us by buying this digital track here.

Our second single, "Exile", is a tune of isolation, hate and disaster. We tell the tale of the repressed emotions that go stale, ultimately turning into something else: an exiled man desperately seeking revenge.

LYRICS

I was the reason you would have lived.
What I tried to give was a cure to all the darkness,
Deaths that were to come.

I've seen enough remains of the wasted.
All the pain that I have tasted...
You thought I was a sinner one so you chose to burn me down.
You sent me into the wild and now I lay exiled.

Lost into the frozen, dark forests, digging for a better path,
As I see my heart turning black,
Searching for a way to release my wrath,
Thinking what I did was wrong.
Was I evil all along?
I tried to save you from a plague...
Why did you do this to me?

I will become your worst nightmare!
My rage shall be upon you!
I am the night, I am the dark that you deserve.

I became the punishing spirit,
I became the evil son of the world,
I became your fear, your death, your destroyer,
I became the plague.

Released on the 1st of September 2020 through DistroKid.
