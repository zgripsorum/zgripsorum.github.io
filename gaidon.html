Gai'don

By Zgripsorum

Buy the digital track here!

In The Old Tongue, "gai'don" refers to a battle between Gods, a key battle, that will win or lose a war. Gai'don is phonetically similar to the word and concept of Armageddon, which inspired the lyrics and the overall intensity and dynamicity of the song (Robert Jordan's "The Wheel of Time").


Lyrics


Renegade the darkness, already gone
We are alone and no one seeks us
Bless the pagan blood, let the shadows rot
Renegade the horror, slaughter remains
Right ahead, the fear avenged us
Sorrow and despair, where light is rare
Full of ash and nowhere else to go
The smoke stuffed our lungs, forgive us
For the harm we caused and the hope we lost
The obscure makes us blind, you see
We are blind, can't see treachery
When the time will come, we will finally overcome

RISE!

When chains finally break, souls are awake
Gloom in their minds and fire in their veins
When our reign suffers in flames
We will remain and our ways we'll regain

And again, the fear has risen, but the blood is still imprisoned
Embrace the dust, our souls combust, our kind is fading so
Listen to the silent stream
There is no cure, we stand up and endure
Conceit remains, and the flood is now red stained
Only pain in our name and home becomes the darkest reign

When chains finally break, souls are awake
Gloom in their minds and fire in their veins
When our reign suffers in flames
We will remain and our ways we'll regain

RISE!

Released July 10, 2020
Mixed & mastered by Marco Doest (Arcana)
