Gai'don

by Zgripsorum

Support us by buying this digital track here.


Our first single, "Gai'don", fuses melodic guitar parts and symphonic elements with the rawness of old school Death Metal, in order to create a chaotic, still controlled new realm. In The Old Tongue, "gai'don" refers to a battle between Gods, a key battle, that will win or lose a war. Gai'don is phonetically similar to the word and concept of Armageddon, which inspired the lyrics and the overall intensity and dynamicity of the song (Robert Jordan's "The Wheel of Time").
